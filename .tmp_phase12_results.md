# CSI Target Reformulation + Quantile Regression — Complete Results

## Phase 1: CSI Target Reformulation

Experiment matrix (LightGBM, t+24h, 171-181 features):

| ID | Target | HRRR | Day nRMSE | Clear nRMSE | Mixed nRMSE | Overcast nRMSE | 
|----|--------|------|-----------|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| C0 | PV power | No (E1) | 0.335 | 0.280 | 0.752 | 1.287 |
| C1 | CSI | No | 0.435 | 0.389 | 0.693 | 1.238 |
| C2 | CSI | Yes | 0.432 | 0.386 | 0.692 | 1.191 |
| C3 | Physical baseline | HRRR-only | 0.614 | 0.514 | 1.214 | 3.124 |
| C4 | CSI | Yes + all | 0.432 | 0.387 | 0.683 | 1.205 |

Key findings:
- CSI improves Mixed (-8%) and Overcast (-7%) at t+24h
- CSI degrades Clear sky (-39%) — because diurnal cycle is the main predictor for clear days
- Overall nRMSE is WORSE for CSI (0.435 vs 0.335) because Clear dominates sample count
- HRRR adds negligible benefit even with CSI target (C1 vs C2: 0.435 vs 0.432)
- Physical baseline (C3) is terrible — ML is needed
- t+6h and t+1h also show CSI degradation
